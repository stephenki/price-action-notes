### 要点 1
> You will not make money if you lose your discipline and take risky trades in the final couple of hours that you would never take in the first couple of hours...

### 要点 2
> Unknown CHAPTER 25 Trading Guidelines Why guidelines and not rules? Because “rules” imply absolutes and easy trading if only you follow them...

### 要点 3
> This can be a trade with a high probability of success and a reward only equal to the risk, one with a low probability of success and a huge reward relative to risk, or anything in between...

### 要点 4
> There is no easy set of reliable rules to make money as a trader, and everything is subjective...

### 要点 5
> A trade cannot have a high probability of making a big reward relative to the risk...

### 要点 6
> You need to be able to follow your rules comfortably, allowing you to enter and exit trades with minimal or no uncertainty or anxiety...

### 要点 7
> You cannot risk two points to make one point and hope to make a profit unless you are right at least 80 percent of the time, and very few traders are that good...

### 要点 8
> When you take a swing trade, you are willing to accept a lower probability of success, but to make money on a scalp, you need a very high probability of success...

### 要点 9
> Until you are consistently profitable, take only trades where your potential reward is at least as large as your risk...

### 要点 10
> To scalp successfully, you usually have to risk about as much as you stand to gain, and that requires that you win on more than 60 percent of your trades...

### 要点 11
> There would quickly be no one left to take the opposite side of the trade, and the rules would then stop working...

### 要点 12
> The smartest thing to do is just make sure that you follow your rules into the close, and you will likely win back some of your losses...

### 要点 13
> The first hour usually has many reversals, so patiently wait for a swing setup, which will generally have less than a 50 percent chance of success but a potential reward that is at least twice as large as the risk...

### 要点 14
> The first hour is the easiest time to lose money as well, because you are overly confident about how easy it might be, and you don't follow your rules carefully...

### 要点 15
> The chance of winning times your potential reward has to be significantly greater than the chance of losing times your risk...

### 要点 16
> Similarly, if you take scalps, but consistently exit early with a profit that is smaller than your risk, you will lose money...

### 要点 17
> Remember, your risk will likely have to be as large as your profit target, so it will usually take six winners just to get back to breakeven after four losses, and this is a very depressing prospect...

### 要点 18
> Realistically, you should scalp only if you can win 60 percent of the time, and most traders should avoid any trade where the potential reward is not at least as large as the risk...

## ⚠️ 常见误区与风险

1. **过早入场**
   - 在信号完全确认前进场
   - 解决：等待收盘确认或下一根 bar 确认

2. **忽视更大时间框架**
   - 只看小时间框架，忽略日线/周线方向
   - 解决：多时间框架协同分析

3. **止损不当**
   - 止损过宽 → 风险回报比差
   - 止损过窄 → 容易被正常波动扫损
   - 解决：基于 swing point 设置

4. **过度交易**
   - 在低概率形态上频繁交易
   - 解决：只做"最佳交易"机会（参考 Ch 24）
